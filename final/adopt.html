Adopt a Friend

When you adopt from Pawsome Animal Shelter, you help save lives and give animals a second chance. Our team will work with you personally to help find a friend that fits your home, schedule, lifestyle, and experience.

The first step in the adoption process requires completing an adoption application. You will then meet the pet in person and speak with a staff member about your expectations and daily routine.

Featured Friends

Lucky | 4-Year-Old Goldendoodle

Lucky is a friendly but timid, medium-energy dog who loves walks and chewing on bones. She does well with small children and calm dogs. Lucky is looking for a family who can give her plenty of attention, love, and daily exercise.

Smudge | 2-Year-Old Tabby Cat

Smudge is a gentle tabby who enjoys chasing toys and quiet naps. He warms up slowly but becomes very affectionate once he feels safe. Smudge would be a great match for a calm home that can give him playtime and attention.
