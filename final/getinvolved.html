Get Involved

Pawsome Animal Shelter depends on volunteers from our community. Whether you like to walk dogs, cuddle cats, or help at events, volunteering makes a real difference in the lives of animals waiting for a friend.

We welcome responsible teens and adults who want to give their time and energy to support rescue animals in our community.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. Fill out a volunteer form in person at Pawsome Animal Shelter or at the local RAC.
  2. Attend volunteer orientation to learn about our rules and safety procedures.
  3. Shadow a current volunteer before working on your own.

To learn more about volunteering with animals, you can also visit the Humane Society Volunteer Information page .
