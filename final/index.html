What We Do

Pawsome Animal Shelter is a community-driven organization committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned or stray animals. We provide temporary housing, medical care, and behavioral support for dogs, cats, and other small animals while they wait for a loving home.

What We Provide

  • Each pet we rescue receives veterinary care, vaccinations, and basic behavior support
  • We help match pets with families based on lifestyle and experience
  • We host adoption events, volunteer days, and programs to promote responsible pet ownership
