GIF Format Example

About This Image

This GIF shows a basketball bouncing continuously, representing motion and energy. The looping animation captures the dynamic movement of the ball — something a still image can’t fully convey.

Why GIF Format?

The GIF format is perfect for short animations like this because it supports looping motion without needing a video player. It also works well for simple graphics with limited colors, making it a good choice for web-friendly motion visuals.
